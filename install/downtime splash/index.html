  • Who are we?
    • Overview
      Dog Company is all about community, as such we pride our selves on being a multinational unit. Dog Company is primarily an Arma 3 unit, but we love to play all sorts games ranging from triple a titles to a little online flash game.
    • Our Goal
      Dog Company's goal is to create a loving and engaging community that gives our members a friendly space to play games and enjoy each others company. We are striving towards becoming the best Arma based mil-sim group in the world.
  • What do we do?
